Who is Jodie Sweetin?

Jodie Lee-Ann Sweetin is a famous American Actress.

She is majorly known for her role as Stephanie Tanner on the sitcom Full House and its spin-off series Fuller House.

Sweetin is also a dancer, singer, and television personality. She first debuted as Stephanie Tanner at the age of five.

She is loved by the audience and she started her career at a very young age.

Jodie Sweetin: Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Sweetin was born on January 19, 1982, in California. Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is English. As of 2022, she is 40 years old.

Her biological parents were in prison. She was then adopted by her uncle Sam Sweetin and his second wife Janice when she was nine months old.

Jodie did not often talk about her adoption publicly out of fear that people would think she was forced into the acting profession.

Education

Jodie Sweetin: Professional Life and Career

Jodie Sweetin’s acting talent was evident early on and, at the urging of family friends, Janice brought her 4-year-old to meet with a talent agent.

She established her career when she was four years old working in a hot dog commercial and did her debut TV appearance on the sitcom Valerie.

Jodie portrayed Leia in the 2012 television film Singled Out.

She turned into a contestant on season 22 of ABC’s Dancing With the Stars.

After this phase in her life, the major peak of her career came. In the TV sitcom Full House,  she played the role of Stephanie Tanner.

This was the most famous role played by Jodie. No wonder why she is known around the globe for this particular role.

After the show ended in 1995, she made a comeback in television by hosting the second season of the Fuse TV show Pants-off Dance-off.
