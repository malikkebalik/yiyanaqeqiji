Who is Cara Geswelli?

Cara Geswelli is a popular TV personality. She is popular for being a cast member of MTV’s top reality show, Siesta Key.

Cara Geswelli: Age, Parent, and Ethnicity

Cara was born on the 14th of March 1995. Her birthplace is New Jersey, the U.S. She grew up with an older sister, Mia Geswelli.

Cara’s father James Geswelli is an inventor. He is also the president of Ultimate Trading Corporation (a jewelry company in New Jersey). Likely, her mother’s name is Jillian Fortunato.

Cara holds an American nationality and has mixed Italian and British descent.

In which school did Cara Geswelli attend?

She graduated with a degree in communication and media studies in 2017 from The University of Tampa. Meanwhile, she was a scholar-athlete in high school.

Cara Geswelli: Career, Professional life

Cara worked at the Dockside Diner in Beach Haven, New Jersey previously. She works there as an Assistant General Manager from April 2016 to September 2018.

Cara came to the limelight after her appearance in the show Siesta Key. She participated in a series and lived there with the young adults. Likewise, she also appears in its second season and it gained her more popularity in 2019.

Eventually, after that, she started working as Human Resource Assistant in the Shauger Group in East Orange, New Jersey.
