Matt Willig is an American actor and a former football offensive tackle who began acting in 1993 and appeared in the film Full Contact as Hulk. He played the role of a special agent, Simon Cade in the 8th season of the police procedural series NCIS. In his sports career, Matt has played for several football teams including Carolina Panthers, New York Jets, Atlanta Falcons, Green Bay Packers, St. Louis Rams, and San Francisco 49ers.
