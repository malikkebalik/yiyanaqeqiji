Debby Ryan is an American actress and singer who is engaged to Twenty One Pilots musician, Josh Dun.

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 16, 2024

Debby Ryan is a famous American Actress and Singer who started her acting career from an early age. She was first seen on the Television on the show Barney and Friends. More to that, she is engaged to reputed American musician Josh Dun, who is one of the band members of Twenty One Pilots.

Debby Ryan was born as Deborah Ann Ryan on May 13, 1993, in Huntsville, Alabama. Debby is the daughter of Chris Ryan and his wife, Sandy Popp Ryan. Debby has a brother named Chase Ryan. Her father was a civilian consultant to the U.S Military. Due to her father's job, Debby lived in various places around Europe.

Debby is Engaged to Twenty One Pilot Musician, Josh Dun

As per Teen Vogue, Debby and Josh announced their engagement on December 22, 2018. The couple made the announcement public by posting a series of photos of their wedding. Among them, one of photo included down on one knee" moment.

Josh first shared the photo on his Instagram account. The engagement was held in a Treehouse in the woods in New Zealand. More to that, Josh surprised Debby, making an unofficial attendance of her brother and sister-in-law.

For the engagement, Josh had bought a stunning three-stone ring. Set in yellow gold, the large emerald-cut diamond is framed by two smaller versions of the stone.

The couple is yet to announce their wedding date. But as per the sources, they are close to finalizing the date for mid-2020. Before getting engaged to Josh, Debby had many ups and downs in her Love life.

Debby Broke up with Josh

Yes, You heard it right. Debby and Josh broke up after dating for almost two years back in 2014. The romantic couple followed a similar path of newlyweds Justin Bieber and Hailey Baldwin.

The love birds reconciled back in 2018 after separating between 2015 to 2017. The relationship has blossomed more than ever and is currently loving each other accompany.

Just for the information, Debby was in a anonymous relationship during her break up with Josh. She mentioned the relationship was very much abusive but hasn't revealed much more about him that has not been said to the media.
