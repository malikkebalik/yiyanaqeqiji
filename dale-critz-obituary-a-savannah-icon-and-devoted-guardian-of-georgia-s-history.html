Dale Critz Obituary, A Savannah Icon And Devoted Guardian Of Georgias History

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Dale Critz Obituary, Death – We mourn the passing of a remarkable individual, Dale Critz Sr., a true Savannah icon, passionate advocate, and avid supporter of Georgia’s rich history. Dale Critz Sr. was not only a dedicated friend of the Georgia Historical Society but also served as a former endowment trustee, leaving an indelible mark on the historical community. Dale’s devotion to his beloved city of Savannah and the state of Georgia was unwavering.

His efforts were dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for everyone, and he strived to leave a lasting impact on the community. Throughout his life, Dale worked tirelessly to make Savannah a better place to live, embracing its unique heritage and advocating for its preservation. In 2019, Dale Critz Sr. and his family made a lasting commitment to Georgia’s history by establishing the Critz Family Fund at the Georgia Historical Society.

This generous act ensured that their dedication to preserving and celebrating the state’s history would endure in perpetuity, leaving an enduring legacy for generations to come. The Georgia Historical Society is deeply grateful for Dale Critz Sr.’s invaluable support and friendship over the years. His contributions have significantly enriched our efforts to protect and promote Georgia’s historical heritage. His vision and passion for preserving the past have left an indelible mark on our organization and the historical community as a whole.

It was an honor to know Dale Critz Sr., and we will deeply miss his presence, guidance, and enthusiasm. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family and friends during this challenging time. May his memory be a beacon of inspiration for all, reminding us of the importance of preserving our history and fostering a sense of community for future generations.
