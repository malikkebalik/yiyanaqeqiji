Matt Coleman from South Jordan passes away unexpectedly: obituary

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 21, 2024

South Jordan, UT – The community of South Jordan, Utah, is in mourning following the unexpected passing of Matt Coleman, a beloved resident. The news was shared via a heartfelt social media post extending condolences to his wife, Brittany Coleman, and their children Brooklyn, Claire, and Hunter.

Matt Coleman, a devoted husband and father, left an indelible mark on the South Jordan community. His dedication to his family, notably his unwavering support and love for his wife and children, defined his life’s purpose. His warm and welcoming nature made him a cherished friend to many, known for his ability to connect with others on a profound level.

In response to this tragic loss, Beau Palmer and Greg Palmer initiated a fundraiser to support Matt Coleman’s family. The fundraiser, aiming to raise $30,000, has already garnered an outpouring of support, reaching $14,169 at the time of this report. The fundraiser, organized through GoFundMe, seeks to assist Brittany and the children during this difficult time.

Jenny Palmer posted on Facebook,

“My heart has been devastated this week by the passing of my sister in laws brother, Matt. My family has been intertwined with the Coleman family for as long as I can remember. This is the last thing we all expected and my heart is hurting soooo much for all the people I love who are missing him so much
“If you are wanting to help his wife and children alleviate some of the burden I included the link to donate.
Skip your Monday coffee and instead send the $5.00 their way. Every dollar counts to allow his wife and kids a little extra time to get adjusted to this new life without Matt here on this earth.
“Tell people you love them. Be kind. People are going through things we know nothing about. A little compassion and love can be huge for someone. And of course… if you are suffering in silence please reach out and get help. No one needs to battle hard things alone. “

The fundraiser’s statement conveyed deep sorrow over Matt’s unexpected passing, emphasizing the love and support extended to Brittany and the children. It highlighted Matt’s profound love for his family and aimed to provide financial aid to ensure their well-being in the wake of his sudden departure.

Details regarding Matt Coleman’s memorial service and obituary are expected to be released by the family at a later date. The family encourages everyone touched by this loss to cherish their loved ones and be mindful of unseen struggles faced by others.
