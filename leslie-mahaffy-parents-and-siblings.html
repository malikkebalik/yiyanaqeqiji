On June 14, 1991, in the dead of night, a horrific encounter irrevocably changed the life of 14-year-old Leslie Mahaffy.

As she walked home, lamenting the untimely death of a schoolmate killed in a horrific vehicle accident, she had no idea that her fate was about to take a sinister and painful turn.

Leslie was at the crossroads of missed curfew and parental retribution when the clock struck 2 a.m.

She sought sanctuary at a neighboring pay phone after being locked out of her residence for violating curfew.

The other end of the line belonged to a friend who, regrettably, refused her refuge for the night.

Leslie returned to her family’s home, ready to make up for her missing curfew.

However, on that fateful night, the innocent adolescent’s life took a horrific and deadly turn when she came into contact with Bernardo, a predator hiding in the shadows.

He kidnapped Leslie, forcing her into an excruciating 24-hour torture beyond imagination.

Leslie was exposed to horrific crimes as hours went into an eternity of torment, the details of which still send shivers down the spines of those who dare to picture her agony.

Her little life was tragically cut short at the hands of her tormentors.

Where Are Leslie Mahaffy’s Parents And Siblings Now?

Despite being severely affected by Leslie’s terrible death, the Mahaffy siblings have found ways to celebrate her memory.
