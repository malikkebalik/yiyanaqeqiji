Taylor Swift Is The Reigning Queen Of Fall Fashion And Heres Proof

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Step aside fashionistas, because Taylor Swift has taken over the world of fall fashion with her impeccable style. From her music to moviemaking and even her recent “collaboration” with the NFL, it’s clear that T-Swift is a pop star who knows how to make an impact. However, her influence doesn’t stop at just her creative endeavours, she’s been captivating fashion enthusiasts with her street style that’s nothing short of perfection.

As she has progressed throughout her career, the newly-minted billionaire has embraced various style eras, eventually finding a distinctive and unique look. Swift’s current fashion aesthetic revolves around elevated classic basics in timeless neutral tones, complemented by sharp accent colours. Also, who can forget the pop queen’s signature red lipstick which ties in all her looks together? If you’re seeking inspiration for your fall outfits, drawing from the ‘Cardigan’ singer’s fashion choices can be an excellent way to channel her iconic style this season.

Fall Outfit 1: The Leather Blazer Marvel

Picture this: Taylor Swift strutting her stuff on the streets of NYC in a cognac-coloured leather blazer from Madewell, paired with burgundy Gianvito Rossi ankle boots and a brown corduroy miniskirt from Acne Studios. It’s a look that screams “fall,” and we didn’t know we needed this Madewell blazer until T-Swift convinced us otherwise. Her outfit is the epitome of fall fashion and it’s bound to sell out faster than a royal-endorsed fashion item. Move over, Barbie; Taylor Swift’s latest fall ensemble is set to revolutionise consumer shopping habits.

Fall Outfit 2: Preppy Perfection

Taylor Swift declared the onset of ‘preppy’ season as she channelled her inner Serena Vanderwoodsen-meets-Rory Gilmore aesthetic and strutted through NYC’s West Village. Swift decided to embrace the no-pants trend but added her unique touch, wearing an oversized Stella McCartney rugby shirt with a horse emblem on the chest. To complete the outfit, she rocked knee-high Prada boots, carried a leather Tod’s shoulder bag and topped it all off with a Ralph Lauren Polo hat. In this look, she’s taken the prep school vibe to a whole new level, tailored for the fall season.

So, if you’re looking for some fall fashion inspiration, look no further than T-Swift. Whether it’s her leather blazer marvel or her preppy perfection, she’s got the secret sauce to nailing those fall looks. It’s officially Taylor Swift’s world, and we’re all just living in it—fashionably.
