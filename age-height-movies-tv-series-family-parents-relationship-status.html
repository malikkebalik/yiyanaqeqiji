Appeared In Netflix's Love: Career Highlight

Moving onto her professional career, Iris Apatow is an actress. She debuted in the movie industry as a child artist. Her first on-screen role was as Charlotte in the 2007 rom-com movie Knocked Up. She appeared opposite her birth mother, and her father made the movie. 

Again in 2012, Apatow performed in the sequel of Knocked Up; This Is 40. She reprised her role as Charlotte. Besides the aforementioned two films, she has also worked in another of her father's directed movie, Funny People in 2009. 

Struggling in the entertainment industry, in 2013, she landed a role in the Netflix original series Love as Arya. The series is an American romantic comedy starring Rust, Mike Mitchell, Claudia O'Doherty, and Gillian Jacobs. 

The role became a breakthrough role for her in the movie industry. Besides that, she has also voiced in Sony Picture's Sausage Party.
