Andrew Nicolson Obituary, Northmavine Fiddle &amp; Accordion Club Member has died

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 24, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Andrew Nicolson Obituary, Death – Today is a day marked by poignant farewells as we come together to bid adieu to a dear friend, Andrew Nicolson, who departed from us on October 10th. Andrew held a significant place within our club, having been a key member from its very inception and taking on the role of a leader for many years. His presence and contributions to the club have left an indelible mark, making his passing all the more poignant.

Andrew’s musical talents graced us at the club’s weekly meetings, where he would play his heart out, infusing life and melody into our gatherings. His music not only filled the rooms but also our hearts, bringing joy and camaraderie to all who were fortunate enough to be in his company. Beyond our club meetings, Andrew’s music resonated at dances and concerts held in various halls and venues throughout Shetland.

His dedication to the art of music made these events special, creating memories that we will forever hold dear. His talents even took us beyond the borders of Shetland to Orkney and Norway, where the beauty of his music transcended boundaries and brought people together. Regrettably, a hand injury a few years ago limited Andrew’s ability to play, causing him immense frustration as he faced this challenge.

Yet, his resilience and determination were a testament to his love for music and his commitment to our club. His presence, even when he could no longer play, remained a source of inspiration for us all. Today, as we say our goodbyes, we do so with heavy hearts. Andrew’s absence leaves a void that cannot be filled, and he will be greatly missed.

His legacy as a musician and leader within our club will continue to echo in our hearts, reminding us of the beauty of music and the importance of coming together in the spirit of harmony and friendship. Our thoughts and deepest sympathies go out to Andrew’s family and friends during this time of loss. As we remember and celebrate the life of a dear friend and musician, may his memory inspire us to continue making music and cherishing the moments we share in the name of our beloved club. Andrew, you will be greatly missed, but your music will forever be a part of our club’s soul.
