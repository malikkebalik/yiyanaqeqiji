Like Mike is a Belgian DJ, record producer, actor, and one half of the very popular brotherly duo Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike. As a part of the duo, he has released various albums and EPs such as Tomorrowland Anthems – The Best of, Bringing the World the Madness, Asia Tour 2016 Edition, Smash the House!, Bringing the Madness, Tomorrowland 2018, and Silence. The duo has also released a wide range of singles which include Tomorrow (Give in to the Night), The Way We See the World, Liquid Skies, Momentum, Madness, Wakanda, Mammoth, Chattahoochee, Tremor, Body Talk (Mammoth), Waves, Nova, Tales of Tomorrow, Melody, Stay a While, Arcade, Hey Baby, He’s a Pirate, Complicated, Ready for Action, The House of House, When I Grow Up, All I Need, Daar gaat ze (Nooit verdiend), and Repeat After Me. Like Mike has also worked with the likes of Gucci Mane, Afrojack, Martin Garrix, Diplo, Ne-Yo, Steve Aoki, Era Istrefi, David Guetta, Wiz Khalifa, Julian Perretta, and Armin van Buuren. Apart from the tremendous success of the duo, Like Mike has also garnered a huge social media fan base for himself with more than a million followers on Instagram and more than 1.2 million followers on Twitter.

First Album

As a part of the duo titled Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, Like Mike released his first compilation album, titled Smash the House!, in February 2010 via DJ Magazine.

In December 2013, the duo released its first soundtrack album, titled Bringing Home the Madness, via CNR Music Belgium.

First Film

Like Mike made his first feature film appearance as himself in the comedy movie, Safety First: The Movie, in 2015. The movie starred actors Matteo Simoni, Bruno Vanden Broecke, and Ruth Beeckmans.

First TV Show

Like Mike made his first TV show appearance in an episode, titled Versuz /Versus, of the drama series, Spitsbroers, in September 2017.

Personal Trainer

He maintains a lean physique by leading an active life and can often be seen working out.

Like Mike Facts

  • He was raised alongside his older brother, Dimitri Vegas, in Willebroek, Antwerp, Belgium.
  • He started his DJing pursuits at a very early age.
  • During the start of his career, he used to perform in several clubs and has also been a resident BeatFM radio DJ along with Dimitri Vegas.
  • Just like his brother, Like Mike has also been portrayed as a star in his very own Tomorrowland comic book written by Paul Jenkins whose credits also include having written for Spider-Man.
  • His grandparents have often attended the concerts of the duo.
  • Like Mike has also worked in the 2017 American swashbuckler fantasy film, Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales.
  • In 2018, he played the role of Harbour Security Agent (as Mike Thivaios) in the crime thriller film, Gangsta.
  • Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike has garnered a huge social media fan base with more than 4 million subscribers on YouTube, more than 4 million followers on Instagram, more than a million followers on SoundCloud, and more than 15 million followers on Facebook.
