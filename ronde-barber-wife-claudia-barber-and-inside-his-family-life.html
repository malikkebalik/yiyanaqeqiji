Ronde Barber Wife Claudia Barber and Inside His Family Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 2, 2024

Ronde Barber wife Claudia Barber is a Marketing Event Coordinator from Florida. Claudia has worked as a diabetic charity organizer.

The former American football player married her lifelong lover in 2001. They celebrated their 20th wedding anniversary in 2021.

Claudia is also a philanthropist who is involved with many charitable organizations. 

Barber played in the NFL for 16 seasons with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers before retiring in 2012. He played his entire professional career as a cornerback and safety.

Afterward, Ronde became an NFL analyst for Fox Sports.

Ronde And Claudia Barber Share Red Carpet

Ronde Barber had a dreamy wedding with Claudia Barber in 2001 with a blend of traditional and modern styles. The two now reside in Keystone, Florida.

After the marriage, they lived in their beautiful mansion in Tampa, Florida. However, they later moved to Keystone in Hillsborough County.

Getty Images has captured some of their moments when they are at events together throughout the years. They were spotted while dining at Waldorf Astoria, New York, during the NCCRA Colon Cancer Benefit program in March 2006.

Similarly, they had a surprising presence at the 7th Gotham Magazines Annual Gala at Capitale in New York in January 2007. Claudia was in a blue one-piece dress and holding a Chanel mini handbag.

They were at an NCAA game at the SunDome Tampa, Florida, in November 2009 when photographer J. Meric clicked their picture.

Ten years after the SunDome picture, Getty uploaded another image of the pair while they attended the Ring of Honor ceremony at Raymond James Stadium in September 2019. The former cornerback Barber looked at her with a smile as they both wore matching red colored dresses for the celebration.

Claudia Barber Works In Charity Services

Claudia is a marketing event planner at Diabetic Charitable Services. She is into charity and humanitarian welfare of Filipino descent.

As an event organizer and coordinator, her assignment is to plan, manage and design programs to raise awareness and fund diabetes research and support services.

Moreover, Claudia helps the association attain its goals and impact diabetes research and aid. Her Linkedin writes she resides in Lutz, Florida.

Ronde Barber Children

Ronde has two amazing children with Claudia. His daughters are named Yammile Rose and Justyce Rosina.

Yammile is the oldest born in May 2022. Barber posted some images of her 20th birthday celebration on his Twitter.

As per her Linkedin, Yammile hit an SAT score of 1560 in December 2018. Moreover, she is also proficient in Korean and Spanish languages.

Ronde's oldest child is a comic book movies fan. She has added various Hollywood production companies to her Interests section, which includes Marvel Studios, Warner Bros Entertainment, Walt Disney, Sony Pictures, Lucasfilm and more.

Rose, with her mom and dad, went to the musical SIX and enjoyed her special day.

Meanwhile, the younger member of the Barber clan, Justyce, a few years younger than her sister Yammile is in her late teens.

She is currently studying Creative writing & Media Arts and Design at the University of Chicago in her third year.

Ronde Barber Family Life

Ronde Barber parents are Daniel X Brack and Geraldine Barber. He grew up in a family of five in Roanoke city of Virginia state.

Ronde has two brothers, Tank and Tiki. Ronde is seven minutes older than twin Tiki. The boys were premature births, five weeks earlier than the due date. 

Every element within the house needed to be a happy and well-nurtured family, but things did not go as planned. His mom Geraldine and dad Daniel divorced when he was a little child.

Father Daniel was a struggling artist who had to stay out of the house a lot because of his work. He barely had time to come home and spend some time with them. Meanwhile, mother Geraldine, who raised all three sons alone, worked two jobs to sustain the family. 

Ronde was a bright kid from the beginning who had a great pull in sports. Geraldine wanted to ensure that her children did not have to experience such hardships, and she had made significant sacrifices for their better life despite all the hard work and scrambling.

Tiki Barber Is A Former NFL Running Back

Tiki is one of the best running backs in the history of the NFL. He retired in 2006 as New York Giants' greatest rusher.

After completing his high school diploma from Cave Spring in Roanoke, Virginia, Tiki joined the University of Virginia on merit. Along with his studies, he was also doing good in athletics. 

Tiki did not start as a football player from his initial days. He had laid a hand on many things. However, football impressed him so much during college that he went for it.

The New York Giants drafted him in the 1997 NFL Draft following his sensational performance. Tiki played his entire career for the Giants for ten full seasons before resting his boots.

After retirement, the 47-year-old joined Television as a sports columnist. Similarly, Tiki is also an author and has several books in his name. 'By My Brother's Side,' Wild Card (2009), and End Zone (2012) are some of his works.

He married his college lover Virginia Cha Barber in May 1999 and now lives on the Upper East Side of NY City. The couple has four children, Chason, AJ, Ella and Riley.
