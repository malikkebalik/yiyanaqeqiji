An American attorney, Angela Rye relationship status seems single after the separation.

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 15, 2024

Angela Rye is a famous American political commentator and political expert. She rose to fame after working for CNN as a political commentator as well as NPR political expert.

Further, she is also the CEO of IMPACT Strategies and works as a senior advisor to the Government Technology and Services Coalition as well. So, being such a multi-professional personality, she must have earned a hefty sum of money until now. Keep reading the article until the end and know about her earnings and net worth.

Short profile of Angela Rye

The famous political commentator and political expert, Angela Rye was born in Seattle, Washington, the U.S. on October 26, 1979. As of 2019, Rye's age is 39 years old. Likewise, she was born as the daughter of (Father) Eddie Rye Jr., a Seattle community activist and Andrea Rye (Mother). Moreover, she belongs to Afro-American ethnicity and holds an American nationality.

Furthermore, she studied at Seattle’s all-girls Holy Names Academy. Later, she joined the University of Washington and graduated from there. Likewise, she completed her higher education from Seattle University School of Law in 2005.

Professional Life

Angela Rye kicked off her career by working as a Western Regional Director for the National Black Law Students Association (NBLSA). Then after, her second job was a Coordinator of Advocacy and Legislative Affairs at the National Association for Equal Opportunity in Higher Education (NAFEO).

Later in 2013, she founded her own political advocacy firm, IMPACT Strategies. Many people praise her works which gave her the chance to appear as a recurring guest at “The Breakfast Club” radio station to talk about updates on the Trump administration. 

Similarly, she has served for House Committee on Homeland security and Congressional Black Caucus for the 112th Congress as well as a political commentator for CNN.

Boyfriend- Common

The 39 years old political commentator, Angela is an unmarried woman; however, she is in a relationship with Common – an American rapper and actor. The couple started dating in the summer of 2017, but the rapper announced their relationship in October.

However, the two of them separated in 2018. As per some sources, the reason for their separation is Common's Love Addiction, and he has to undergo therapy for a while.
