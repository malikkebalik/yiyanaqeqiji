Chief of Spanish Football, Luis Rubiales, Faces Doubled Child Support Payments Due to Ex-Wifes Co

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Reports indicate that suspended Spanish Football Association chief, Luis Rubiales, has been directed to increase his child support payments following legal action by his ex-wife amid a controversial kissing scandal.

The 46-year-old father of three, who is currently under scrutiny for an unsolicited kiss on World Cup-winning player Jenni Hermoso, is said to have been mandated to double the amount of his previous payments due to a significant increase in his income.

It has been reported by Spanish TV station La Sexta that a court in Valencia ordered Rubiales to raise his monthly payments for each of his daughters from 400 to 800 euros.

His ex-wife, identified as Maria Manuela Delicado Vega, is reported to have requested a revision of the child support payments after Rubiales’ income surged from 97,000 euros in 2011 to 955,000 euros in 2020.

Despite his current legal and personal challenges, Rubiales had previously spoken in a TV interview about his daughters, their strengths, and his relationship with his ex-wife.

It was also revealed that he had a good relationship with his ex-wife and expressed that they had always prioritized their daughters.

The decision regarding increased child support payments was made by Valencia’s Provincial Court on June 19, predating Rubiales’ suspension as the Spanish FA president.

It is yet to be seen how his suspension will impact the amount he is required to pay in child support.

Rubiales’ ex-wife is known to reside in a modest house in a small town near Valencia with at least one of her daughters.
