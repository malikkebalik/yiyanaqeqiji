Biography

Richard Pryor was born in Peoria, IL on December 1, 1940. Uncompromising African-American comedian, actor, and writer known for his cutting-edge and often controversial stand-up material. He also starred in See No Evil, Hear No Evil; The Toy; and Brewster’s Millions. His body of recorded work includes the comedy albums Richard Pryor: Live & Smokin’ and …Is It Something I Said? Jerry Seinfeld once called him the Picasso of the comedy profession. After being expelled from school at age fourteen, he performed as a drummer in night clubs.
