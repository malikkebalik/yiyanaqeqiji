Hailey Bieber trolled for 'hideous' footwear choice as fans notice her big feet in photoshoot: 'Not

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 15, 2024

BEVERLY HILLS, CALIFORNIA: Hailey Bieber, known for her impeccable style, has rightfully earned the title of fashion queen. Nevertheless, her recent photos have sparked controversy and caused division within her fan base.

In a recent Instagram post, Hailey shared a collection of photos titled "Home." However, the internet disagreed with her choice of shoes and trolled Hailey for her "disgusting" shoes.

Hailey Bieber shares 'home' photo series

Showcasing her personal sanctuary, Hailey delighted her Instagram followers with a captivating series of photos aptly titled "Home" Each photo was a testament to her unrivaled sense of style, as she effortlessly showcased a plethora of chic ensembles. The visual treat included delectable snapshots of a cake adorned with the name 'Hailey' and delicious pancakes that seemed almost too perfect to eat. But the true essence of the series was Hailey's impeccable fashion sense, which took center stage.

View this post on Instagram

 

In one of the photos, Hailey is wearing an oversized bomber jacket that exudes an air of casual cool. Paired with a mini dress and elegant heels, it was a fusion of edginess and femininity. Another snap showed her fearless approach to fashion as she wore shorts with stylish socks and strappy shoes. Amid the overwhelming admiration for her fashion choices, however, fans paid attention to one particular detail: her unconventional shoe choice. Her post attracted attention not only for its adorable esthetic, but also for the strange juxtaposition of shoes and outfit. Critics spoke out, accusing Hailey that her shoes did not match her carefully put-together ensembles. The controversy sparked a passionate debate among her followers, who were divided on whether her daring fashion choice was an expression of artistic freedom or an unfortunate fashion faux pas.

'Get rid of those hideous shoes please' 

Hailey's choice of shoes immediately drew criticism from the online community, with vocal opinions flooding in. One individual exclaimed, "The jorts!?! The socks and sandals!?!?!? No. Please. No," expressing their disdain for the unconventional combination. In a rather direct manner, another commenter questioned, "Why yo feet so big?" Highlighting their love for Hailey while expressing dissatisfaction, one person voiced, "I happen to love Hailey!! I just think her shoes are disgusting, sorry for my opinion." Taking notice of the apparent sizing issue, someone pointed out, "You have a veeery big foot in these shoes..." As the comments poured in, some expressed that Hailey's shoe game was going from bad to worse, "The shoes get worse." Echoing a sentiment of resistance, another person chimed in with a sense of disbelief, saying, "Oh nooo, we're not doing the socks and sandals era again ... nooo ma'am."
