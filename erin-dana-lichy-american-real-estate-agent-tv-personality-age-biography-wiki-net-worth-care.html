Professional Career

Erin Dana Lichy was well-versed in the New York City real estate world as a child. It was due to the fact that both of her parents were actively interested in the field.

Eventually, her desire to become an agent grew stronger with time. As a result, at the age of 19, she became a real estate agent. She began purchasing and selling properties throughout the city while still in university.

According to her Linkedin page, she was a very creative individual who founded her own handbag company, Erin Dana LLC, in March 2014. As a result, she began selling women’s handbags across the country.

She began working as a Creative Director for Framework Fashion in December 2016. However, it is thought that she quit her employment and that her bag firm is no longer in operation. It was because she was entirely preoccupied with real estate.

She eventually became an agent with the real estate firm Douglas Elliman in December 2018. She joined Eklund Gomes’ team at the organization roughly two years ago. The previous year, the group completed over $4 billion in transactions.

In January 2019, she established Homegirl, an interior design firm. The company specializes in property renovation and design. As a real estate agent, she finds it quite easy to find consumers in this industry.

In terms of her television career, she made her debut as a guest on the show Million Dollar Listing New York in 2018. She also appeared as a guest on two additional episodes of Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen (2022) and Entertainment Tonight (2023).

Her most recent television appearance was on Bravo’s The Real Housewives of New York City. She appeared in the 14th season alongside five other celebrities, including Brynn Whitfield, Jenna Lyons, Sai De Silva, and others.
