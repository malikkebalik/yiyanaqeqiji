Find Out Zeeko Zaki's Net Worth from Showbiz

The American actor and model, Zeeko Zaki is well known for his role as Aimon Shah in the action television series NCIS: Los Angeles. Similarly, he is famed as Omar Adom “OA” Zidan in a crime drama television series FBI.

With a sucessful career, Zeeko not only gained a high recognition, but the guy is also earning a reasonable sum of money and is living a lavish lifestyle. To know more about him, read the entire article.

Total Fortune – $500 Thousand

Zeeko Zaki is earning a reasonable sum of money from his professional acting life. As of 2019, his estimated net worth is around $800 Thousand.

Zaki played on many TV shows and movies from where he gained not only an impressive sum of wealth but also enough prominence, love, and support.

In 2015, the actor played in computer-animated comedy film Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Road Chip, which was made under the budget of $90 million and collected $234 million at the box office. In the film, he shared screen alongside Josh Wiggins and Lauren Graham.

Zeeko starred in 2016 American dystopian romantic thriller film, The Divergent Series: Allegiant, which collected $179.2 million at the box office. In the movie, he starred side by side Theo James, Jeff Daniels, and Miles Teller.

Supportive Parents

Grew up in West Chester, Pennsylvania, the actor spent his childhood alongside his brother and sister.

Zaki’s mom manages a salon, whereas his father is a stylist. His mother wanted him to become an actor, but he wasn’t a bit interested in acting. Even though he was not excited, he did make his mother happy. Now, look how sucessful he is!

The handsome guy attended Unionville High School from where he graduated. After that, he became a member of community theater group Unionville Players, where he acted. Zeeko also attended Temple University where he studied business.
