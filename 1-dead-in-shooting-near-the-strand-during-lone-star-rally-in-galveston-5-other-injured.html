1 dead in shooting near the strand during Lone Star Rally in Galveston, 5 other injured

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 6, 2024

Tragedy struck the Lone Star Rally in Galveston, Texas, as law enforcement agencies responded to reports of a mass casualty incident late Saturday night. Initial reports indicated that the incident, which escalated into a shooting, arose from an altercation between bikers on the streets. The situation quickly spiraled out of control, leading to a chaotic scene and numerous injuries.

Over a dozen law enforcement officials are currently involved in the ongoing investigations, with the Galveston Police Department confirming their swift response to the shooting incident at the intersection of 21st and Mechanic around 11:30 P.M. Emergency personnel worked tirelessly to attend to the injured individuals, with at least seven victims transported to various hospitals with gunshot-related injuries.

The identities of the injured victims have yet to be disclosed, as authorities continue their efforts to clear the scene and gather pertinent information. Victims were found at various locations in the vicinity, including Moody and Strand, as well as 21st and Harborside. The GPD is actively securing the Strand and downtown area, while advising the public to exercise caution and remain vigilant.

This is a developing story, with law enforcement agencies dedicated to uncovering the details surrounding this tragic incident at the Lone Star Rally. As the community grapples with this unsettling turn of events, authorities urge anyone with relevant information to come forward and assist with the ongoing investigation.
