Donald Campbell Wiki Biography

Born as Don Campbell in Chicago, Illinois USA, on 30 November 1950, Don “Magic” Juan is a famous bishop and hip hop personality. He spent his early years as a pimp, before turning to faith and becoming a preacher. He is also an actor and accomplished fashion designer.

Have you ever wondered how much Don “Magic” Juan is worth, as of late 2016? According to authoritative sources, Juan’s net worth is estimated to be around $300,000, mostly attributed to his previous career as a pimp, as well as his acting, fashion, and hip hop involvements.

Bishop Don Magic Juan Net Worth $300,000

Juan grew up on the streets, and by age 16 he had entered the world of pimping. Juan was heavily influenced by people like Iceberg Slim, and films like “Superfly” (1972), and he took to the pimp lifestyle naturally, becoming very popular not just in Chicago, but across the country. He owned a record store in Chicago that doubled as a front for his prostitution business, and certainly provided the basis to his net worth.

On 6 February 1985, everything changed for Don Juan; a little less than 20 years after getting into the pimp business, Juan wanted out. He claims to have received a vision from God, urging him to drop his lifestyle of sin and debauchery, and Juan took the vision very seriously and shut down his pimping business. He went to Dr. Leonard P. Rasher, who schooled him in gospel, and eventually he was ordained as a minister by Dr. F.L. Johnson.

In 1989, Juan decided to take his new-found spirituality and open the Magic World Christian Kingdom Church of the Royal Family; he simply wanted to help as many people as he possibly could. Although not as lucrative as being a pimp, Juan has made a decent living from being an Archbishop, and his net worth is constant.

Although that’s not the only thing that attributes to it; besides preaching and helping people, Juan has acted as a spiritual advisor for various celebrities, including Snoop Dogg. He has also been featured on many hip hop albums and music videos, including by artists like 50 Cent, B-Real, and Twista. Juan hasn’t just cameoed in hip hop either; he has been in a variety of films, usually appearing as himself. All of these appearances, and using his newfound fame, have further increased his net worth, as well as helping even more people.

Juan has also appeared in various documentaries about the pimp lifestyle, being very open on the topic. He is an author too, having written extensively about his life in his autobiography. His main focus is still studying scripture and helping communities across the country. Although being a preacher doesn’t add much wealth to his net worth, it still gives him a purpose in life.

Regarding his personal life, Juan is apparently not married and has no children. He dedicates the majority of his time to being an Archbishop in Chicago, although he still likes to cameo in various films or albums if the chance presents itself.
