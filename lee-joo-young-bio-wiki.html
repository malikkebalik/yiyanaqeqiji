Who is Lee Joo-Young?

Lee Joo-Young is a well-known South Korean actress, model, and social media celebrity with millions of followers on her many social media platforms.

She is well-known for earning honors like the Wildflower Film Awards and the KBS Drama Awards, and she is handled by the Ace Factory.

Lee Joo Young is noted for appearing in the television series ‘Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-Joo’, ‘Times’, and ‘Itaewon Class’. She was most recently seen in Green Night, One Win, and Rose Mansion. She is also working on her next project, Dojeon 2, and Deal.
