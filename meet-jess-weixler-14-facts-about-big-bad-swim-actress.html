Early Life

Jess was born on June 8, 1981, in Louisville, Kentucky, USA as the only child of her father, Mark Weixler, and mother, Donna Emerick.

She was interested in acting from an early age, and she also got full support from her parents in her interest. During her days in school, she joined various groups such as The River City Players acting group and the Chamber Singers choral group to learn acting.

Education Qualification

In the Death of Dick Long, actress Jess completed her high school education at Atherton High School in Louisville, Kentucky.

After completing her high school education, she went to Walden Theatre Conservatory Program to get her bachelor's degree in theater arts and drama.

Moreover, she also attended The Juilliard School along with famous American actress Jessica Chastain, who has won an oscar award for her role in The Eyes of Tammy Faye. They both graduated from school at the same time.

Career Overview

Jess Weixler started her on-screen acting career with a small role in the Guiding Light TV series, where she portrayed the role of Caroline Boyle.

In the same year, she again appeared in the crime-drama TV series Hack as Martha Skolnick. Since then, she has appeared in several other movies and TV series. Some of her famous movies and TV series are Little Manhattan, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, Somebody Up There Likes Me, and Apartment Troubles.

Currently, she is working on the upcoming horror-thriller movie Hell House. Moreover, she has just finished working in Cabin Girl and In Sickness & Health.
