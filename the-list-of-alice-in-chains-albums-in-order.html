Alice in Chains Albums in Order

Since their formation in 1987, Alice in Chains has become one of the most influential and successful bands in the history of rock music. With their unique blend of heavy metal, grunge, and alternative rock, the band has consistently delivered powerful and haunting music. Here is the list of their albums in chronological order:

  • Facelift (1990): The debut studio album that introduced Alice in Chains to the world. It featured the hits “Man in the Box” and “Bleed the Freak,” and quickly established the band as a force to be reckoned with in the grunge scene.
  • Dirt (1992): Regarded as one of the greatest albums in rock history, Dirt showcased Alice in Chains’ dark and introspective side. With tracks like “Rooster” and “Would?,” the album explored themes of addiction, depression, and mortality.
  • Jar of Flies (1994): Released as an EP, this acoustic-driven record became the first EP to debut at number one on the Billboard 200 chart. It contained the acclaimed single “No Excuses” and displayed the band’s versatility and depth of songwriting.
  • Alice in Chains (1995): Often referred to as the “Tripod” album due to its cover art, this self-titled release marked a departure from their earlier sound, embracing a more alternative and experimental approach. It included the hit “Grind.”
  • Black Gives Way to Blue (2009): The band’s first album after a long hiatus following the tragic death of lead vocalist Layne Staley, it marked the debut of new frontman William DuVall. The title track was a heartfelt tribute to Staley, and the album solidified Alice in Chains’ enduring legacy.
  • The Devil Put Dinosaurs Here (2013): This album displayed the band’s continued ability to create heavy, brooding music. It featured the hit single “Hollow” and received critical acclaim for its dark sound and strong songwriting.
  • Rainier Fog (2018): The latest Alice in Chains album at the time of writing, Rainier Fog showcased the band’s ability to maintain their signature sound while exploring new musical territories. It debuted at number 12 on the Billboard 200 chart.

    • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    1. How many albums has Alice in Chains released?

    Alice in Chains has released a total of seven studio albums.

    2. Which Alice in Chains album is considered their breakthrough?

    The album Facelift is often regarded as Alice in Chains’ breakthrough, as it introduced the band to a wider audience and established them as a prominent force in the grunge movement.

    3. What is Alice in Chains’ most successful album to date?

    Their second studio album, Dirt, is their most successful commercially, having achieved multi-platinum status and receiving widespread critical acclaim.

    4. Who are the founding members of Alice in Chains?

    The founding members of Alice in Chains are Jerry Cantrell (guitar, vocals) and Sean Kinney (drums). The original lineup also included Mike Starr (bass) and Layne Staley (vocals).

    5. Which album marked the return of Alice in Chains after their hiatus?

    Black Gives Way to Blue marked the return of Alice in Chains after a hiatus of over a decade, with William DuVall taking over vocal duties alongside Jerry Cantrell.

    6. Has Alice in Chains won any Grammy Awards?

    Yes, Alice in Chains has won two Grammy Awards. They won Best Hard Rock Performance for the songs “Man in the Box” and “Again,” both featured on the album Facelift.

    7. Did Alice in Chains undergo any lineup changes over the years?

    Yes, there have been changes in the lineup of Alice in Chains. Mike Inez replaced Mike Starr on bass in 1993, and William DuVall joined the band as co-lead vocalist in 2006 following Layne Staley’s death.

    8. What are some of Alice in Chains’ most popular songs?

    Some of their most popular songs include “Man in the Box,” “Rooster,” “Would?,” “No Excuses,” and “Nutshell.” These songs have become anthems of ’90s rock music and continue to resonate with fans today.

    9. How would you describe Alice in Chains’ musical style?

    Alice in Chains’ musical style is a unique blend of heavy metal, grunge, and alternative rock. They are known for their dark and haunting sound, characterized by heavy guitar riffs, harmonized vocals, and introspective lyrics.

    10. Have Alice in Chains influenced other bands?

    Undoubtedly, Alice in Chains has had a significant influence on many bands in the rock and metal genre. Their fusion of alternative and metal elements, along with their emotionally charged songwriting, has inspired countless musicians.

    11. Do Alice in Chains still perform live?

    Yes, Alice in Chains continues to perform live and tour regularly. They have a strong and dedicated fan base that eagerly attends their shows to witness their intense and passionate performances.

    12. Are there any upcoming albums or projects from Alice in Chains?

    At the moment, there haven’t been any official announcements regarding upcoming albums or projects from Alice in Chains. However, fans eagerly anticipate new music from the legendary band.

    By providing this list of Alice in Chains albums, along with frequently asked questions and answers, fans and newcomers alike can gain a comprehensive understanding of the band’s discography and legacy. Alice in Chains’ dark and mesmerizing music continues to captivate listeners, solidifying their place in the annals of rock history. As Jerry Cantrell once said, “Music is the last true voice of the human spirit. It can go beyond language, beyond age, and beyond color straight to the mind and heart of all people.”1
