Revengers Shack Elden Ring, Where to Find Revengers Shack Elden Ring Location?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Revenger’s Shack Elden Ring

Revenger’s  Shack is a location in Elden Ring. It is found in the Western Liuirnia region of the Lakes. This location can be reached by going towards the North in the Minor Erdtree. The Revenger’s Shack guide shall provide the information on where to get Revengers’s Shack and its SItes of Graces, Merchants, Weapons, Items, and points of interest within the location.

Revenger's Shack Location 

The Revenger’s Shack is found in the Luirnia of the Lakes region. The exact location is West Liurnia of the Lakes in the North of Minor Erdtree. 

  • Region - Liurnia of the Lakes

  • Spot - West Liurnia of the Lakes, north of the Minor Erdtree 

Revenger's Shack is located just north of the Minor Erdtree in southwest Liurnia.
