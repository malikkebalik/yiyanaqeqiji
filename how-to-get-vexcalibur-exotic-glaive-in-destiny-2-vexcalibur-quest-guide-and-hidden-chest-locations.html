  • Complete the “Presage” mission on any difficulty to unlock Master difficulty.

  • Reach a Power level of at least 1325 before attempting the Master difficulty version of the mission.

  • Assemble a fireteam and enter the Master difficulty version of the “Presage” mission.

  • Progress through the mission until you reach the final boss, and defeat it.

  • After defeating the final boss, open the hidden chest in the boss room to obtain the Catalyst for the Vex Mythoclast.

  • Return to orbit and complete the Catalyst by killing enemies with the weapon in PvE activities.

  • Once the Catalyst is complete, return to the “Presage” mission on Master difficulty and defeat the final boss again to obtain the Vex Mythoclast.
